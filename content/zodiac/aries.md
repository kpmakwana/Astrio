## Characteristics

Aries is the first sign of the zodiac, representing new beginnings and fresh starts. People born under this sign are known for their leadership qualities, courage, and pioneering spirit.

### Positive Traits
- Bold and courageous
- Natural leaders
- Enthusiastic and energetic
- Independent and self-reliant
- Honest and direct

### Challenges
- Can be impulsive
- May lack patience
- Tendency to be self-centered
- Quick to anger
- May rush into decisions

## Career
Aries individuals excel in leadership roles, entrepreneurship, and competitive fields. They thrive in environments that allow them to take initiative and make quick decisions.

## Relationships
Aries are passionate and direct in relationships. They value honesty and appreciate partners who can match their energy and independence.

## Health
Aries rules the head, so they should pay attention to headaches, migraines, and head-related issues. Regular exercise helps channel their abundant energy.
