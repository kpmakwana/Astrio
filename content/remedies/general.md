# General Astrological Remedies

## Mantras

**Repetitions:** 108 times  
**Best Time:** Sunrise  
**Benefits:** Enhances confidence, leadership, and vitality

### Moon Mantra
**Mantra:** Om Chandraya Namaha  
**Repetitions:** 108 times  
**Best Time:** Evening  
**Benefits:** Improves emotional balance and mental peace
